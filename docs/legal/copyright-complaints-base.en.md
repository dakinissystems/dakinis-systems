# Copyright & intellectual property complaints

**Last updated:** 19 May 2026

## Scope

For content hosted on Dakinis services (e.g. AkoeNet, user messages, shared files).

## Form

**AkoeNet:** https://akoenet.dakinissystems.com/legal/dmca

## By email

**legal@dakinissystems.com** — subject: «Copyright / IP complaint»

## Minimum information

- Identification of the rights holder or authorized agent
- URL or location of allegedly infringing content
- Description of the protected work
- Good-faith statement that use is not authorized
- Contact details

## Process

We review the notice and, where appropriate, remove or restrict content. We may request additional information.

> For services with users in the U.S., we also accept DMCA-style notices; the AkoeNet form includes standard fields.
