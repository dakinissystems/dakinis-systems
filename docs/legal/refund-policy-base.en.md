# Refund policy (Dakinis ecosystem)

**Last updated:** 19 May 2026

## 1. Scope

Applies to subscriptions and licenses for **Dakinis One**, **StreamAutomator**, **LifeFlow**, and other products billed by Dakinis Systems via Stripe.

## 2. Renewal

Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled from the product dashboard or Stripe customer portal.

## 3. Refunds

Unless required by law, **billed periods are non-refundable**. Cancel before renewal to avoid future charges.

## 4. Billing errors

Duplicate or incorrect charges: **billing@dakinissystems.com** within **14 days** of the charge.
