# Privacy requests channel (GDPR)

**Last updated:** 19 May 2026

> **Note:** We do not appoint a data protection officer. This channel is our **privacy contact** for exercising your rights.

## Contact

**Email:** privacy@dakinis-systems.com

**Form (AkoeNet):** https://akoenet.dakinissystems.com/legal/privacy-requests

## Rights you may exercise

- Access
- Rectification
- Erasure («right to be forgotten»)
- Objection
- Restriction of processing
- Data portability (copy in a structured, commonly used format)

## How to request

1. State the right you wish to exercise and the product (corporate site, Dakinis One, AkoeNet, StreamAutomator, etc.).
2. Provide a verifiable contact email.
3. We may request reasonable proof of identity before fulfilling the request.

## Timeframes

We respond within applicable legal deadlines (typically **one month**, extendable under GDPR).

## Supervisory authority

You may lodge a complaint with the **Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD)**: https://www.aepd.es
