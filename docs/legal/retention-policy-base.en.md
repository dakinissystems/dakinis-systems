# Data retention policy

**Last updated:** 19 May 2026 · **Controller:** Christian David Villar Colodro (Dakinis Systems) · Tax ID (NIF) 18513473Z

Indicative periods; may vary due to legal duty, litigation, or security needs.

| Category | Period |
|----------|--------|
| **Account data** | While the account remains active; deletion or anonymization after confirmed closure |
| **Security logs** | Up to **12 months** |
| **Support communications** | Up to **24 months** |
| **Billing records** | According to applicable tax obligations in Spain |
| **Moderation / DSA records** | As long as needed to handle the case and meet legal duties |
| **Backup copies** | Operational backup cycle; purged per procedure |
