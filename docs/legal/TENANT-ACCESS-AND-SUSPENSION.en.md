# Tenant access and suspension (Dakinis One)

**Last updated:** 19 May 2026

**Operator:** Christian David Villar Colodro · **Trading name:** Dakinis Systems · **Tax ID (NIF):** 18513473Z

## 1. Scope

This document describes access, downgrade, and suspension rules for **Dakinis One** tenant accounts (core.dakinissystems.com), applicable to all business types (restaurant, clinic, salon, real estate).

## 2. Access

- Each tenant operates in an isolated environment identified by `business_id`.
- Access requires valid credentials and, where applicable, an active commercial plan.
- Users are responsible for keeping their credentials confidential.

## 3. Downgrade for non-payment

- Non-payment may downgrade the contracted plan until payment is regularized (effective Starter plan).
- Advanced features (AI, WhatsApp, Pro modules) may be limited during downgrade.

## 4. Suspension and closure

Dakinis Systems may suspend or close accounts for:

- Serious breach of the terms of use
- Service abuse or unauthorized access attempts to other tenants
- Legal or regulatory requirement
- Fraudulent or unlawful use

Where reasonable, the tenant administrator will be notified by email before or concurrently with suspension.

## 5. Data export

Before permanent closure, the tenant may request data export in a structured format by writing to **privacy@dakinissystems.com**, subject to identity verification.

## 6. Reactivation

Accounts suspended for non-payment may be reactivated upon payment regularization. Other suspensions require manual review by contacting **legal@dakinissystems.com**.
